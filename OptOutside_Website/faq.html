Where are the best hiking spots? Do I need any special training?

This is really dependent on your preference. For example, you can find great hiking trails in the mountains, along the coast, or even the desert. The training is also dependent on your ability level. There are many easy to intermediate level hikes if your are just starting out. If you are looking for more of a challenge, check out our selection of hikes to do. Either way, it is always a good idea to prepare yourself to be ready for the right level of your next hike. If it's Yosemite, the Grand Canyon, or even a hike in your local park, use this site to help you find great local hikes in your area or to prepare and train for your next great bucketlist hike!

Do I need to make special reservations for my next adventure?

It is always a good idea to check ahead of time on the requirements. For example, going for a hike in the mountains or state parks may require you to purchase a day pass or parking permit whereas visiting Havasu Falls will require you to make a special reservation in advance with the local Native Supai tribe. There are many adventures and activities however, that do not require you to make any special reservations. Check out the latest details before heading out on your next excursion to make your outing as enjoyable as possible.
